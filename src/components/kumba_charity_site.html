How KUMBA Works

💫

Automatic Giving

A small percentage of every KUMBA transaction automatically goes to verified charities. No extra steps, no hassle - just automatic impact.

🌍

Global Impact

Support causes worldwide including education, clean water, healthcare, and environmental protection. Your transactions create ripple effects of positive change.

🔒

100% Transparent

Every donation is tracked on the blockchain. See exactly where your contribution goes and the impact it creates in real-time.

🤝

Community Driven

KUMBA holders vote on which charities to support. Democracy meets philanthropy in the most transparent way possible.

⚡

Low Fees, High Impact

Minimal transaction fees mean maximum donations. Built on efficient blockchain technology to ensure your money goes where it matters most.

📊

Real-Time Tracking

Watch your collective impact grow with live dashboards showing donations, beneficiaries reached, and projects funded.
